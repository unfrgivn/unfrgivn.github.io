### Problem
Rigid order forms and checkout flows were a constant roadblock for marketing and sales teams, limiting the creativity of their campaigns and requiring engineering intervention for changes.

### Goal
Allow marketing teams to self-serve and customize the order checkout experience without sacrificing the security and resiliency required for capturing orders.

### Criteria
- Integrate with the legacy subscription fulfillment system.
- Allow for full customization of payment methods and gateways.
- Make forms embeddable into any customer-facing application.
- Push customization capabilities to non-technical users.

### Outcome
- **Revenue Growth**: Abandoned cart rates dropped by ~15%, and online sales revenue increased by 20% annually through multi-step upsell/cross-sell flows.
- **Empowerment**: Created customizable checkout templates within marketing and CMS platforms.
- **Extensibility**: Extended the previously rigid entity structure of the Subscription API and ARM application to support offer and product customization.
